### **Fundamentals of Communication Systems Tutorial 6**

**1. Suppose we have an audio signal. How to convert it into a digital audio signal?**
**1. 假设我们有一个音频信号。如何将其转换为数字音频信号？**

*   **English:** To convert an analog audio signal into a digital audio signal, a process called Pulse-Code Modulation (PCM) is typically used, which involves three main steps:
    *   **Sampling:** The analog audio signal is sampled at regular intervals. According to the Nyquist theorem, the sampling rate must be at least twice the highest frequency in the audio signal to be accurately represented.
    *   **Quantization:** The amplitude of each sample is measured and assigned a numerical value from a predefined range of discrete levels.
    *   **Coding:** The quantized values are then converted into binary code, resulting in a stream of digital data that represents the original analog audio signal.
*   **中文:** 要将模拟音频信号转换为数字音频信号，通常使用一种称为脉冲编码调制（PCM）的过程，该过程包括三个主要步骤：
    *   **采样：** 以固定的时间间隔对模拟音频信号进行采样。根据奈奎斯特定理，采样率必须至少是音频信号中最高频率的两倍，才能被准确表示。
    *   **量化：** 测量每个样本的幅度，并从预定义的离散电平范围中为其分配一个数值。
    *   **编码：** 然后将量化后的值转换为二进制码，从而得到代表原始模拟音频信号的数字数据流。

**2. Once analogue audio is converted into digital audio, how to transmit it over the air?**
**2. 一旦模拟音频被转换为数字音频，如何通过无线方式传输它？**

*   **English:** Once the audio is in a digital format, it can be transmitted wirelessly using the following steps:
    *   **Channel Coding:** Error correction codes are added to the digital data to protect it from errors that may occur during transmission.
    *   **Modulation:** The digital data stream is then used to modulate a carrier wave.
    *   **Transmission:** The modulated carrier wave is amplified and sent to an antenna for transmission over the air as a radio wave.
*   **中文:** 一旦音频为数字格式，就可以通过以下步骤进行无线传输：
    *   **信道编码：** 在数字数据中添加纠错码，以保护其在传输过程中免受可能发生的错误的影响。
    *   **调制：** 使用数字数据流来调制一个载波。
    *   **传输：** 将调制后的载波放大并发送到天线，以无线电波的形式在空中传播。

**3. We want to digitize the human voice containing frequency from 0 to 4000 Hz. What is the bit rate, assuming 8 bits per sample?**
**3. 我们想要数字化频率范围为0到4000赫兹的人声。假设每个样本为8比特，比特率是多少？**

*   **English:** To calculate the bit rate, we use the following formula: *Bit Rate = Sampling Rate × Bits per Sample*
    1.  **Determine the Sampling Rate:** According to the Nyquist theorem, the sampling rate must be at least twice the highest frequency.
        *   Sampling Rate = 2 × 4000 Hz = 8000 samples per second.
    2.  **Calculate the Bit Rate:**
        *   Bit Rate = 8000 samples/second × 8 bits/sample = 64,000 bits per second (bps) or 64 kbps.
*   **中文:** 我们使用以下公式计算比特率： *比特率 = 采样率 × 每样本比特数*
    1.  **确定采样率：** 根据奈奎斯特定理，采样率必须至少是最高频率的两倍。
        *   采样率 = 2 × 4000赫兹 = 8000次采样/秒。
    2.  **计算比特率：**
        *   比特率 = 8000次采样/秒 × 8比特/采样 = 64,000 比特/秒 (bps) 或 64 kbps。

**6. Explain the following terms: FDMA, TDMA, CDMA.**
**6. 解释以下术语：FDMA, TDMA, CDMA。**

*   **English:**
    *   **FDMA (Frequency Division Multiple Access):** In FDMA, the total available frequency band is divided into several smaller, non-overlapping frequency channels. Each user is allocated a unique frequency channel for the duration of their communication.
    *   **TDMA (Time Division Multiple Access):** In TDMA, users share the same frequency channel but take turns using it. The channel is divided into time slots, and each user is assigned a specific time slot to transmit or receive data.
    *   **CDMA (Code Division Multiple Access):** In CDMA, all users transmit on the same frequency at the same time. However, each user is assigned a unique digital code that distinguishes their signal from others.
*   **中文:**
    *   **FDMA (频分多址):** 在FDMA中，总的可用频带被划分为几个更小的、不重叠的频率信道。每个用户在通信期间被分配一个唯一的频率信道。
    *   **TDMA (时分多址):** 在TDMA中，用户共享同一个频率信道，但轮流使用它。信道被划分为多个时间片，每个用户被分配一个特定的时间片来发送或接收数据。
    *   **CDMA (码分多址):** 在CDMA中，所有用户在同一时间、同一频率上传输。但是，每个用户都被分配一个唯一的数字码，用于区分彼此的信号。

**7. Assume that there are four stations in total, connected to the same channel...**
**7. 假设总共有四个站连接到同一信道...**

*   **English:** To prove that a receiving station can get the data sent by a specific sender, let's consider the properties of the orthogonal codes used in CDMA. Let the data from the four stations be d1, d2, d3, and d4, and their corresponding orthogonal chip codes be c1, c2, c3, and c4.
    The signal on the channel will be the sum of the data from all stations multiplied by their respective codes:
    *Channel Signal = (d1 * c1) + (d2 * c2) + (d3 * c3) + (d4 * c4)*
    Now, let's say station 2 wants to receive the data from station 1 (d1). Station 2 will multiply the entire channel signal by station 1's code (c1):
    *Result = [(d1 * c1) + (d2 * c2) + (d3 * c3) + (d4 * c4)] * c1*
    *Result = (d1 * c1 * c1) + (d2 * c2 * c1) + (d3 * c3 * c1) + (d4 * c4 * c1)*
    Due to the properties of orthogonal codes: The product of a code with itself (c1 * c1) results in the number of stations (4), and the product of two different orthogonal codes (e.g., c2 * c1) is 0.
    Applying these properties:
    *Result = (d1 * 4) + (d2 * 0) + (d3 * 0) + (d4 * 0) = 4 * d1*
    Finally, the receiving station divides this result by the number of stations (4):
    *(4 * d1) / 4 = d1*
    This demonstrates that the receiving station can successfully recover the data (d1) from the specific sender (station 1).
*   **中文:** 为了证明接收站能够获取特定发送方的数据，我们需要考虑CDMA中使用的正交码的特性。假设四个站的数据分别为d1, d2, d3, d4，它们对应的正交码片为c1, c2, c3, c4。
    信道上的总信号是所有站的数据与它们各自码片相乘后的总和：
    *信道信号 = (d1 * c1) + (d2 * c2) + (d3 * c3) + (d4 * c4)*
    现在，假设站2想要接收站1的数据(d1)。站2会将整个信道信号与站1的码片(c1)相乘：
    *结果 = [(d1 * c1) + (d2 * c2) + (d3 * c3) + (d4 * c4)] * c1*
    *结果 = (d1 * c1 * c1) + (d2 * c2 * c1) + (d3 * c3 * c1) + (d4 * c4 * c1)*
    根据正交码的特性：一个码片与自身的乘积(c1 * c1)等于站的数量(4)，而两个不同的正交码片相乘(如 c2 * c1)等于0。
    应用这些特性：
    *结果 = (d1 * 4) + (d2 * 0) + (d3 * 0) + (d4 * 0) = 4 * d1*
    最后，接收站将这个结果除以站的数量(4)：
    *(4 * d1) / 4 = d1*
    这证明了接收站可以成功地从特定发送方（站1）恢复出其数据（d1）。
